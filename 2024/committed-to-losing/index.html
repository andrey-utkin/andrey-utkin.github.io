Committed to losing

September 22, 2024 One-minute read
humor

We’re the good guys. We are always ready to sacrifice ourselves for all the good stuff we love in the world.

But we never get to enjoy our life!

It’s all their fault. They’re the bad guys - authoritarian countries committing genocides and other atrocities, companies harming the society and planet with their sick unsustainable practices, and simply criminals.

They tell us to tie our own hands or they’ll kill our beloveds whom they hold hostages.

We know they never hold their promises.

But we have to tie our hands, because we love our beloved.

These bastards killed them! And now they are going to kill me. How dare they be so evil!

It’s all their fault though.
