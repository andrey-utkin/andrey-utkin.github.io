Olga Pulatova released 'Darkness'

July 31, 2024 One-minute read

Today my favourite musician Olga Pulatova released her new music album “Darkness”.

Listen, pay, download here: https://pulatova.bandcamp.com/album/--5

For me she is a charismatic and witty goth pianist & singer. She is from Odesa and became famous across Ukraine and beyond, in Fleur band, where many of their best songs were her. Her most popular older hits are perhaps Silkworm (Шовкопряд) and Formalin (Формалін).

Enjoy!
