MathTime!

October 30, 2023 One-minute read
math • kids

For the last few weeks my family is up to our ears in maths exercises and brain teasers as my daughter has developed a big interest.

I am setting up a “newsletter” for puzzles. Once in a few days I would send a few exercises. Readers can send their solutions if they want, and I will mark them. This would cost a few pounds per month.

To sign up, email to mathtime@autkin.net with your kid(s) age, grade and goals.
