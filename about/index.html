About me

I am a professional software developer.

Since the start of my career, I chose to work with systems based on Free and Open Source Software. I have never regretted this choice. The freedoms to use, learn, change and share the code always were important in helping me meet the ever changing needs of myself and others - friends, customers and employers. I am deeply thankful to those who made it possible. I do my best to contribute back and introduce new people to the treasures of FOSS.

I am continuously active in public on GitHub as andrey-utkin.

I use my PGP key to sign code and emails when required.

You are welcome to email me via andrey_utkin@fastmail.com .
